Susie

People hate seeing a girlboss win

Susanna Patrya Haltmann, usually referred to as Susie, is the secretary and administrative assistant of the Haltmann Works Company. In Kirby: Planet Robobot, she was introduced as an antagonist. Susie is the daughter of Haltmann, the president of Haltmann Works Company.

Likes: ice cream, singing karaoke, working hard

Dislikes: losing, intellectually inferior individuals

Before Kirby: Planet Robobot (Backstory)

Susie's father, Haltmann, performed tests on Star Dream, a machine he reactivated. Unfortunately, there was an accident during one of the tests and Susie was transported to another dimension.

Years later, Susie somehow made her way back from the other dimension and interviewed for the Haltmann Works Company. Haltmann, despite no longer having memories of her due to all of his Star Dream tests, felt Susie was familiar and decided to hire her as Haltmann Works Co.'s executive assistant and secretary. Is it truly nepotism at work if Haltmann doesn't remember who Susie is?

With Susie now part of Haltmann Works Co., President Haltmann put her in charge of the company's Mechanizing Occupation Project.

During Kirby: Planet Robobot

The Haltmann Works Company invaded Planet Pop Star after making it the next target of the company's Mechanizing Occupation Project.

Kirby first meets Susie at Overload Ocean, where she formally introduces herself to him and explains why the invasion is happening. Susie tells Kirby that Pop Star's natives take the planets resources for granted, and that Kirby has to be destroyed. The two fight, with Kirby prevailing. Susie leaves.

Kirby encounters Susie again, this time in Gigabyte Grounds. Susie tells him the story about the time she met a powerful knight, which turned out to be Meta Knight. It turns out that Susie captured Meta Knight and turned him into a cyborg. Susie has Kirby fight Mecha Knight, Meta Knight's cyborg form, and leaves.

Kirby encounters Susie yet again at Rhythm Route. She greets Kirby and releases Dedede Clone, leaving Kirby to fight it.

Susie is encountered again inside of the Access Ark. Susie scolds him for entering the place as if it was his own living room. She has Kirby fight Mecha Knight+, an upgraded version of Mecha Knight, and leaves.

Susie is surprised that Kirby managed to defeat Mecha Knight twice. She gets ready to fight Kirby once more, but is interrupted by President Haltmann. Kirby defeats President Haltmann, who then access Star Dream. Just as Haltmann is about to put on Star Dream's control helmet, Susie comes in and takes the helmet. She reveals her secret plan: to steal Star Dream and sell its database to a start-up company for a large profit. Before she is able to do this, Star Dream becomes sentient and knocks Susie unconscious after blasting her with a laser.

After President Haltmann is possessed by Star Dream, Susie urges Kirby to stop Star Dream. After Star Dream is eventually defeated and the mechanized planets are reverted back to normal, Susie then flies off into space.
