Susie

People hate seeing a girlboss win

Susanna Patrya Haltmann, usually referred to as Susie, is the secretary and administrative assistant of the Haltmann Works Company. In Kirby: Planet Robobot, she was introduced as an antagonist. Susie is the daughter of Haltmann, the president of Haltmann Works Company.

Likes: ice cream, singing karaoke, working hard

Dislikes: losing, intellectually inferior individuals

Before Kirby: Planet Robobot (Backstory)

Susie's father, Haltmann, performed tests on Star Dream, a machine he reactivated. Unfortunately, there was an accident during one of the tests and Susie was transported to another dimension.

Years later, Susie somehow made her way back from the other dimension and interviewed for the Haltmann Works Company. Haltmann, despite no longer having memories of her due to all of his Star Dream tests, felt Susie was familiar and decided to hire her as Haltmann Works Co.'s executive assistant and secretary. Is it truly nepotism at work if Haltmann doesn't remember who Susie is?

With Susie now part of Haltmann Works Co., President Haltmann put her in charge of the company's Mechanizing Occupation Project.
